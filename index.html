About Me

Chef Ravi Patel

I'm Chef Ravi Patel, hailing from the vibrant and culturally rich country of India. My culinary journey began in the bustling streets of Delhi, where the enticing aromas of spices and simmering dishes first ignited my passion for cooking. With a deep appreciation for diverse flavors, I am proud to bring my love for food to life through my top recipes: buttery Butter Chicken, hearty Lasagna, and flavorful Beef Shawarma.

Throughout my career, I've had the honor of refining my skills and showcasing the beauty of Indian cuisine fused with other culinary traditions. My Butter Chicken is a crowd favorite, bursting with rich spices and creamy goodness, while my Lasagna adds a twist of Indian flair with spices that dance on your palate. The Beef Shawarma, with its succulent marinated meat and vibrant toppings, reflects my love for street food and global flavors.
