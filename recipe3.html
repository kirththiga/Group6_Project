Beef Shawarma Recipe

  • For the marinade:
    • 500g beef (thinly sliced, preferably sirloin or flank steak)
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
    • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
    • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • For the accompaniments:
    • Pita bread or flatbread
    • Tahini sauce or yogurt-garlic sauce
    • Sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions
    • Pickled turnips or pickled cucumbers
    • Fresh parsley or cilantro (chopped)
  1. Marinate the beef: In a bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients. Add the beef slices, ensuring they’re well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).
  2. Cook the beef: Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated beef slices for 2-3 minutes on each side until they’re browned and tender.
  3. Assemble the shawarma: Warm the pita bread or flatbread. Place a generous portion of the cooked beef in the center. Add your choice of vegetables, pickles, and a drizzle of tahini or yogurt-garlic sauce. Sprinkle with fresh parsley or cilantro.
  4. Wrap and enjoy: Roll up the bread tightly around the filling, and your shawarma is ready to serve!
